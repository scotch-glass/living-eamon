# SH 11.2 — On the Lamp

*A mosaic on the chapel floor, half-hidden under the altar carpet. The lamp-tender lifts the carpet only for those who know how to ask.*

Light has no enemy but neglect.

In the upper world, Mind is light without form — pure, unbodied, wholly itself. In the lower world, light is Mind taking shape: a flame, a lamp, a steady-burning oil. Tend the lamp and you tend a piece of God. Let it gutter and you let a piece of God grow lonely.
