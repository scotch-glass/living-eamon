# SH 21.6 — On the All as One

*Etched along the spine of a book whose title has been deliberately scraped off. Master Orin says the title was the danger; the etching is only the door.*

The cosmos is one living thing.

Its parts know each other as your hand knows your other hand — wordlessly, without effort. The forgetting came later, with the body, with the name. The work of the seeker is not to learn this. It is to remember it.

> As above, so below. As within, so without.
