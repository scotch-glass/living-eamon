Anchored on the historical 42 Negative Confessions of the Egyptian Book of the Dead, recast in Living Eamon's voice as positive affirmations — the *path*, not the avoidance.*

## On the form

The historical 42 Negative Confessions were declarations of *what one had not done* in life, recited before the 42 Assessors of Ma'at in the Hall of Two Truths. Living Eamon recasts them as **the path**, not the avoidance — what one walks toward, rather than what one merely refrained from.

The 42nd oath is the Hall of Two Truths arrival. It spans all six PICSSI dimensions because at the moment of weighing, all of them are weighed at once.
